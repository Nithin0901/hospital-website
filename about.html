Overview

St. Joseph of Cluny Multispecialty Hospital is a Unit of the Charitable Society of the Sisters of St. Joseph of Cluny Congregation originated in France-Cluny, having its headquarters at Paris. It is registered under Society Registration Act SR263/1998. It is a charitable, private, voluntary organization started in the year 1978, the Cluny Sisters took up our residence in CLUNY BHAVAN and began our ministry on 4th May 1978, at Chennai, in view of providing quality health care at low cost to all the people, with a preferential option for the poor and downtrodden of the society. Women and children are our special concern.
